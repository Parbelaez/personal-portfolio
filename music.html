Nerd, for real!
Eng / Spa

When I was 7 years old, my parents gave me a keyboard on which I learned popular Latin American and Caribbean songs. But, let's be honest: a kid who listens to Guns n' Roses, Metallica, Poison, among others, there is little enjoyment in learning those songs, not to mention the instrument when the dream is to emulate Slash.


At the age of 12, I started playing guitar and the keyboard disappeared from my life... at least for many years. I went through several bands, until 2005 when I got a call from Johnie All Stars, the band of several childhood friends and with whom I played constantly until 2014, composed 3 albums and still collaborate remotely in the composition of new music.


For some months now, I have been writing a collaborative album with several musician friends, with no pressure and no specific release date. I also write the music for much of the audiovisual material I work on personally.
