Cinema is my great passion. Cinema is addictive. Cinema lives in my head day and night, either as an accumulation of ideas of stories I would like to tell through this medium or as an endless list of movies to watch.


Ever since I was a kid I wanted to see every movie that came out and I wouldn't take my eyes off the TV on Saturdays, when HBO would release the movie of the week or Showtime Fridays.


Once in college, I discovered film festivals and specialized publications, which along with music magazines, I devoured immediately after the mail arrived with them.


When I started writing about cinema, I was already working in a cinema club presenting films that were hard to find in pre-internet Medellín. And it is this same Internet and the arrival of multimedia that changed everything. Blogs, videos and editing software opened an infinite horizon of possibilities.


Nowadays, I make personal videos as a hobby, I write scripts with the intention of selling them someday or producing them myself through Urua Films GmbH, the production company I work for and the one that will release "Hasta Que se Apague el Sol", a documentary in which I have worked in the production and some technical aspects.
