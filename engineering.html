I am a musician since I was 7 years old and my childhood dream was to be an audio engineer. The problem: the profession did not exist in Colombia ... the solution: study electronic engineering and then opt for a scholarship for a master's degree in audio.


But sometimes life just happens. The need for music was supplied while playing in my band: Johnie All Stars. My job as a Telecommunications Engineer was stable, and my other passion, writing, was becoming more and more prominent.


After 9 years of working in all the technical positions that a cellular network can have, I decided to get to know the world through my profession. I then went to work as a consultant in several countries as a mobile network optimizer, until I ended up in Berlin, where after a year working in network access, I took the position of crowdsourcing project manager. Something completely new that introduced me to the IT world. And finally, I developed the home internet benchmarking system for Germany, which consists of 3 products: a probe (HW), a comparative speed-test (SW), and a crowdsourcing data analysis (data delivery with more than 1.5MM users).


I am currently developing a new project based on a web app for the movie industry and estimated to be released in early 2024.
